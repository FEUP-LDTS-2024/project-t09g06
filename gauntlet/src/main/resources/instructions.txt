                              GAME INSTRUCTIONS


 welcome to the 2d map adventure game! In this game, you take on the role

 of a heroic character navigating a dangerous map while avoiding monsters

            utilizing strategic skills, and overcoming obstacles

                                 OBJECTIVE


 survive as long as possible by avoiding monsters, managing your resources,

  and using your skills strategically. earn points by outsmarting the

                        monsters and staying alive

                                 CONTROLS


movement: use the arrow keys or (w,s,a,d) to move your hero across the map

shoot: press the spacebar to shoot bullets

                                  TIPS


    -> when shooting, to hero will shoot in the last direction you moved,

         so plan ahead your movement to not be caught defendless

    -> you have limited ammo, so use your shots wisely

    -> there is a reload time after using your bullets



                        back to menu - press 'q'
